The \(x\) coordinate of \(\vec{r}(t)\) is \(t\text{,}\) the \(y\)-coordinate is \(e^t\) and the \(z\)-coordinate is \(-t^2\text{,}\) so

\begin{align*} \vec{F} (\vec{r}(t)) \amp =- te^t\vec{i} +\sin ( t) \vec{j} -(t^2)^2\cdot t\vec{k}\\ \amp =- te^t\vec{i} +\sin ( t) \vec{j} - t^5\vec{k}. \end{align*}
