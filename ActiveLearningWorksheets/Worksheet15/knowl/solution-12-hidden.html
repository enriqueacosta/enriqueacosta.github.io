To parameterize a curve that follows that graph of a function \(y=f(x)\) oriented from right to left, we can choose \(x=-t\) and then we get \(y=f(-t)=-t^3\text{.}\) Thus, \(\vec{r}(t) =-t\vec{i} -t^3\vec{j}\) is a vector equation for \(y=x^3\) oriented from right to left. Since \(x=-t\) we choose \(-2\leq t\leq 1\) to restrict to the appropriate domain.
