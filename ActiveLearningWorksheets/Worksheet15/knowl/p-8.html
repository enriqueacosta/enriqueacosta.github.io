A vector equation for the line in the direction of \(\vec{v}=2\vec{i} +2\vec{j} -\vec{k}\) passing through the point \(P=(2,2,2)\) at \(t=0\) is \[\vec{r}(t)=\vec{P}+t\vec{v},\] where \(\vec{P}\) is the position vector with head at point \(P\text{.}\) Thus we get

\begin{align*} \vec{r}(t)\amp =2\vec{i}+2\vec{j}+2\vec{k}+t(2\vec{i} +2\vec{j} -\vec{k}) \\ \amp =(2+2t)\vec{i} +(2+2t)\vec{j} +(2-t)\vec{k}. \end{align*}
