Solution 2.2.1.

The vector pointing from \((1,2,3)\) to \((3,2,1)\) is \(\vec{v}=2\vec{i} -2\vec{k}\text{.}\) Therefore, a vector equation for this line segment will be

\begin{equation*} \vec{r}(t)=\vec{i} +2\vec{j} +3\vec{k} +(2\vec{i} -2\vec{k})t=(1+2t)\vec{i} +2\vec{j} +(3-2t)\vec{k} \end{equation*}

with \(0\leq t\leq 1\text{.}\)
