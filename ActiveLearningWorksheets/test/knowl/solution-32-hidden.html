\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} \text{Value of first investment}\amp = {10000} e^{{0.041} t} \amp \\ \text{Value of second investment}\amp = {5000} e^{{0.087} t} \amp \\ \text{so}\quad {5000} e^{{0.087} t}\amp = {10000} e^{{0.041} t} \amp \text{set values equal} \\ \frac{e^{{0.087} t}}{e^{{0.041} t}}\amp = \frac{{10000} }{ {5000} } \amp \text{divide} \\ e^{{0.087} t-{0.041} t}\amp = 2.000 \amp \text{simplify} \\ e^{{0.046} t}\amp = 2.000 \amp \\ {0.046} t\amp = \ln 2.000 \amp \text{take logs} \\ t\amp = \frac{\ln 2.000 }{{0.046} } \\ \amp = {15.068} , \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

so it will take about \(15\) years.
