Solution 9.1.1.

Let \(t \) be the doubling time. Then the population is \(2P_0 \) at time \(t \text{,}\) so

\begin{align*} 2P_0 \amp = P_0 e^{{ }t} \\ 2 \amp = e^{{ }t} \\ { } t \amp = \ln 2 \\ t \amp = \frac{\ln 2}{{ }}\approx .\ \end{align*}

So, the doubling time is about \(\) years.
