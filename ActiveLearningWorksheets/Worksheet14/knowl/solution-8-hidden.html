To parameterize a curve that follows that graph of a function \(y=f(x)\text{,}\) we can choose \(x=t\) and then we get \(y=f(t)=2t^2-1\text{.}\) Thus, \(\vec{r}(t) =t\vec{i} +(2t^2-1)\vec{j}\) is a vector equation for \(y=2x^2-1\text{.}\) Since \(x=t\) we choose \(-1 \leq t\leq 2\) to restrict to the appropriate domain.
