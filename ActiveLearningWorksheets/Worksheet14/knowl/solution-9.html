Solution 1.2.4.1.

To parameterize a curve that follows that graph of a function \(x=f(y)\text{,}\) we can choose \(y=t\) and then we get \(x=f(t)=\sin t\text{.}\) Thus, \(\vec{r}(t) =\sin t\vec{i} +t\vec{j}\) is a vector equation for \(x=\sin y\text{.}\) Since \(y=t\) we choose \(0\leq t\leq 2\pi\) to restrict to the appropriate domain.
