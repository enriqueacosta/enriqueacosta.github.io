The vector pointing from \((1,2)\) to \((-1,3)\) is \(\vec{v}=-2\vec{i} +\vec{j}\text{.}\) Therefore, a vector equation for this line segment will be

\begin{equation*} \vec{r}(t)=\vec{i} +2\vec{j} +(-2\vec{i} +\vec{j})t=(1-2t)\vec{i} +(2+t)\vec{j} \end{equation*}

with \(0\leq t\leq 1\text{.}\)
