Definition of trigonometric functions using right triangles.

For an angle \(\theta\) in a right triangle (not the right angle):

\begin{equation*} \sin\theta=\frac{\text{Opposite}}{\text{Hypotenuse}},\qquad \cos\theta=\frac{\text{Adjacent}}{\text{Hypotenuse}},\qquad \tan\theta=\frac{\text{Opposite}}{\text{Adjacent}}. \end{equation*}
