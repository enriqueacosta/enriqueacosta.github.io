See the figure above. Since the tangent is the length of the opposite side divided by the length of the adjacent side,

\begin{equation*} \tan58^\circ=\frac{d}{50} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} d=50\tan58^\circ\approx80.017 \end{equation*}

The width of the river is about 80 meters.
