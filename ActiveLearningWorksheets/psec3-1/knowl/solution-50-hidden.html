The zeros are \(x=3 ,5\text{,}\) so \(y=a(x-3 )(x-5 )\text{.}\) Solving for \(a\text{,}\) we have

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} a(0-3 )(0-5 )\amp =\amp 2 \\ 5 a\amp =\amp 2 \\ a\amp =\amp \frac{2 }{5}, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

so \(y={\frac{2}{5}\!\left(x-3\right)\!\left(x-5\right)} \text{.}\)
