Solution 6.22.1

Since the parabola has \(x\)-intercepts at \(x=- \) and \(x= \text{,}\) its formula is:

\begin{equation*} y=a(x+ )(x- ). \end{equation*}

The coordinates \((, )\) must satisfy the equation, so

\begin{equation*} =a(+ )(- ). \end{equation*}

Solving for \(a\) gives \(a=\frac{}{}\text{.}\) The formula is:

\begin{equation*} y=\frac{}{} (x+ )(x- ). \end{equation*}
