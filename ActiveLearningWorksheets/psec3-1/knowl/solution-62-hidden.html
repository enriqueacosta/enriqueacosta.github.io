The zeros are \(x=2 ,4\text{,}\) so \(y=a(x-2 )(x-4 )\text{.}\) Solving for \(a\text{,}\) we have

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} a(0-2 )(0-4 )\amp =\amp 5 \\ 4 a\amp =\amp 5 \\ a\amp =\amp \frac{5 }{4}, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

so \(y={\frac{5}{4}\!\left(x-2\right)\!\left(x-4\right)} \text{.}\)
