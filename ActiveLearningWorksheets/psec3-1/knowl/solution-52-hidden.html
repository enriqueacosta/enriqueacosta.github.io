Since the parabola has \(x\)-intercepts at \(x=-8\) and \(x=1 \text{,}\) its formula is:

\begin{equation*} y=a(x+8 )(x-1 ). \end{equation*}

The coordinates \((2, 10)\) must satisfy the equation, so

\begin{equation*} 10=a(2+8 )(2-1 ). \end{equation*}

Solving for \(a\) gives \(a=\frac{1}{1}\text{.}\) The formula is:

\begin{equation*} y=\frac{1}{1} (x+8 )(x-1 ). \end{equation*}
