Solution 4.1.1

Setting the factors equal to zero, we have

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} {2}-x\amp =\amp 0\\ x\amp =\amp {2}, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} {9}-{8} x\amp =\amp 0\\ x\amp =\amp {9}/{8}, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

so the zeros are \(x={2},{9}/{8}\text{.}\)
