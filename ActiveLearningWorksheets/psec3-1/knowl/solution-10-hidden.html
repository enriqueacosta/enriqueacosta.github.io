Setting the factors equal to zero, we have

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} {6}-x\amp =\amp 0\\ x\amp =\amp {6}, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} {5}-{4} x\amp =\amp 0\\ x\amp =\amp {5}/{4}, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

so the zeros are \(x={6},{5}/{4}\text{.}\)
