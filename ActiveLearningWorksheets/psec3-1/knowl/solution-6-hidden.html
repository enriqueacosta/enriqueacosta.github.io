Setting the factors equal to zero, we have

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} {4}-x\amp =\amp 0\\ x\amp =\amp {4}, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} {3}-{2} x\amp =\amp 0\\ x\amp =\amp {3}/{2}, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

so the zeros are \(x={4},{3}/{2}\text{.}\)
