Solution 4.2.1

We have

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} y\amp =\amp {3} ({0.4} x-4)(4-20x)\\ \amp =\amp {3} ({0.4} )(x-{10})(-20)(x-4/20) \\ \amp =\amp -{24} (x-{10})(x-0.2), \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

so \(k=-{24} ,r={10},s=0.2\text{.}\)

Note that the order of \(r\) and \(s\) does not matter.
