Solution 5.5.1

Setting the factors equal to zero, we have

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} {8}-x\amp =\amp 0\\ x\amp =\amp {8}, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} {8}-{7} x\amp =\amp 0\\ x\amp =\amp {8}/{7}, \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

so the zeros are \(x={8},{8}/{7}\text{.}\)
