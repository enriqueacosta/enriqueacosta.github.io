We solve for \(r\) in the equation \(Q(r)={3} r^2-{3} r-{168}=0\) using the quadratic formula with \(a={3}\text{,}\) \(b=-{3}\) and \(c=-{168}\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} r\amp =\amp \frac{-(-{3}) \pm \sqrt{(-{3})^2-4({3})(-{168})}}{2({3})}\\ r\amp =\amp \frac{{3} \pm \sqrt{{9}+{2016}}}{{6}}\\ r\amp =\amp \frac{{3} \pm \sqrt{{2025}}}{{6}}\\ r\amp =\amp \frac{{3} \pm {45}}{{6}} \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

Therefore \(r=({3} + {45})/{6}={8}\) and \(r=({3} - {45})/{6}=-{7}\text{.}\) The zeros of \(Q(r)\) are \(r={8}\) and \(r=-{7}\text{.}\)
