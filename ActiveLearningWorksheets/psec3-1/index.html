This worksheet is meant to be used in class. You should be working in groups or individually depending on the instructions from your teacher.

You can submit answers any number of times.

Remember that the point is that you learn from your mistakes and with the help of your peers, so make sure you reflect on incorrect answers and try again with your new understanding. If you can't figure it out, ask your teacher, or bring up your strategy in a whole class discussion. This will allow you to get as much out of the activity.
