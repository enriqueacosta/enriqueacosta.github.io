The cylinder \(4=x^2+y^2\) has contours which consist of concentric circles centered on the \(z\)-axis whose radii are all equal to \(2\text{.}\) Therefore, it can be parameterized by letting \(z=v\text{,}\) \(x=2\cos{(u)}\) and \(y=2\sin{(u)}\text{,}\) or \(\vec{r}(u,v) =2\cos{(u)} \vec{i} +2\sin{(u)} \vec{j} +v\vec{k}\text{.}\)
