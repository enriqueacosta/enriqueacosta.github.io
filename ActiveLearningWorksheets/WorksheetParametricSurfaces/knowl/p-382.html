Since \(x=u\text{,}\) \(y=v\) and \(z=\sqrt{u^2+v^2}\text{,}\) in order to have \(z\leq 4\text{,}\) we must have \(\sqrt{u^2+v^2}\leq 2\) or equivalently \(u^2+v^2\leq 4\text{.}\) This is all points within the circle \(u^2+v^2\leq 4\text{,}\) and thus can be bounded by either \(-2\leq u\leq 2\) and \(-\sqrt{4-u^2} \leq v\leq \sqrt{4-u^2}\text{,}\) or \(-2\leq v\leq 2\) and \(-\sqrt{4-v^2} \leq u\leq \sqrt{4-v^2}\text{.}\)
