First, \(S\) can be parameterized by \(\vec{r}(u,v)=u\vec{i} +v\vec{j} +(2-u-v)\vec{k}\text{.}\) Since we want to restrict to the first octant. we need \(x=u\geq 0\text{,}\) \(y=v\geq 0\text{,}\) \(z=2-u-v\geq 0\) which gives the bounds \(0\leq u\leq 2\) and \(0\leq v\leq 2-u\text{.}\) The normal vector is \(\vec{N} =\vec{i} +\vec{j} +\vec{k}\) and so \(||N||=\sqrt{3}\text{.}\) Thus we have

\begin{align*} \int \int_{S} xz \, dS \amp =\int_{0}^{2} \int_{0}^{2-u} u(2-u-v) \sqrt{3} \,dv\,du\\ \amp =\sqrt{3} \int_{0}^{2} \int_{0}^{2-u} (2u-u^2-uv) \,dv\,du \\ \amp =\sqrt{3} \int_{0}^{2} \left( 2uv-u^2v-u\frac{v^2}{2} \right)\bigg|_{0}^{2-u} \,du \\ \amp =\sqrt{3} \int_{0}^{2} \left( \frac{u^3}{2} -2u^2+2u \right) \,du \\ \amp =\sqrt{3} \left( \frac{u^4}{8} -\frac{2u^2}{3}+u^2 \right)\bigg|_{0}^{2} \,du \\ \amp =\sqrt{3} \left( \frac{2^4}{8} -\frac{2(2)^2}{3}+2^2 \right)\bigg|_{0}^{2} \,du \\ \amp = \frac{2\sqrt{3}}{3}. \end{align*}
