Tikz 3D - Twisted Cubic

Mon, Dec 6, 2021 There was this section on Twisted Cubics and so I decided I wanted to make a Tikz picture for it. Hours later I ended up with the code and figure which I attached, which I am really proud of! I am becoming a huge Tikz fan.
